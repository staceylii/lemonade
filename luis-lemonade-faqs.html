FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Background

Who owns Luis' Lemonade?

Luis' Lemonade is a privately held company. Barry and Kayla are co-CEOs.

Is Luis' Lemonade a minority seller?

Yes, Luis' Lemonade is a certified National Minority Supplier.

How can I contact Luis' Lemonade’S Corparate Office?

You are welcome to call 1-512-580-3899 or email us at Hello@luislemonade.com

Product Related

Where is your product made?

Luis' Lemonade is made in a commercial food processing facility in the Marion, Virginia area.

Is Luis' Lemonade pasteurized?

Yes, our lemonade is pasteurized. It is heated to an elevated temperature for a very short amount of time to ensure food safety.

Do I need to keep Luis' Lemonade refrigerated?

Luis' Lemonade requires refrigeration after opening.

Buying Luis' Lemonade

Where can I buy Luis' Lemonade?

You can find our lemonade at a wide variety of corner store all over New York City. To find a retailer in your neighborhood, please visit our Store Locator.

Can I order your lemonade online?

Unfortunately our products are only sold in stores.

Does Luis' Lemonade work with a distibutors?

Yes, we currently partner with United Natural Foods. If you are interested in distributing Luis’ Lemonade, please send us an email to lemonade.luis@gmail.com
